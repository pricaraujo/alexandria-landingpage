The Problem

Parents receive thousands of conflicting recommendations online every day. Reliable information exists, but it is often scattered across multiple trusted sources, making it difficult to find the right guidance at the right time.

🔍 Information Overload

Parents often spend hours searching through articles, videos, forums, and social media trying to understand what's best for their child.

🏥 Trusted Knowledge is Scattered

Evidence-based guidance from organizations like the NHS and WHO is available, but spread across many websites and documents.

❤️ Confidence Matters

Our goal is to transform trusted healthcare information into simple, personalized guidance that grows with every child.
